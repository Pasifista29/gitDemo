A] https: Protocol
    >> set of rules/instrutions
        http: Used to transfer web pages on the internet. Not secure; data is sent in plain text.
        https: Secure version of HTTP. Uses encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data during transfer.
        tcp: Reliable, connection-oriented protocol. Ensures all data arrives correctly and in order.
        udp: Fast, connectionless protocol. No guarantee of delivery; used for live streaming and gaming.
        smtp: Protocol used for sending emails between mail servers.
        ftp: Used for uploading and downloading files over a network.
        ip: Responsible for assigning addresses and routing data from one device to another on the internet.
